Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Skill Census Project?

The Skill Census Project is an initiative aimed at mapping the skillsets within our community to better understand and address workforce needs.

How can I participate in the survey?

You can participate in the survey by visiting our Survey Page and filling out the form provided.

What will be done with the data collected?

The data collected will be analyzed to identify skill gaps and opportunities, and to create visualizations and reports that can benefit the community and policymakers.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, your personal information is safe. We take privacy seriously and ensure that all data is securely stored and used only for the purposes of this project.
